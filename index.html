T he government passed the Act in 15 July 2001 to establish a science and technology university in Pabna. The university will be located in the district head quarter of Pabna. Pabna is a central district town in northern Bangladesh having long historical and cultural heritage. The academic curriculum of the Pabna University of Science and Technology was started on 05 June 2008. This university will play an innovative role in providing need-based higher education, training and research. Also the university will be committed to maintain and raise the quality and standard of higher education for the students as in international standards. This practical and need-based curricula will produce highly qualified trained scientists and technologists for the needs of Bangladesh as well as the world employment market. The university offers education in science- and technology-based subjects for undergraduate and post graduate levels. This newly established university will occupies an area of about 30 acres, with a number of multi-storied buildings. Because of its location, it has already drawn the attention of brilliant students for admission and scholarly faculty members for teaching and research.

Vice Chancellor's Message

Welcome Message from the Vice-Chancellor

I am really pleased to introduce Pabna University of Science and Technology (PUST) one of the leading pilgrimages of higher educations in the northern part of Bangladesh established in 2008. The sincere age suited decision taken by the Government for establishing this university has become a significant landmark for digitalization and promotion of technology based higher education even in the remote areas which is really appreciable. PUST aims at ensuring modern science and technology based education in high standard educational environment to generate skilled and quality manpower that will grow as the creative member of the society. It upholds research and creation of new knowledge as a supreme interest for its teachers and students in all fields including social arts and Business disciplines too. Keeping the challenges of 21st century, it mobilizes the growth and skills for our young generation. The university hopes to produce enlightened future leaders with adequate scientific knowledge, social norms, ethics and values. I always believe that people make a university great. We strongly welcome all forms of progressive ideas with all steps together. I expect that one day, the name of this university would be placed at the frontier in the international arena by promoting technological development and strengthening related research. It will be a place where great research figures will stand and history will be made. I do welcome our well-wishers at our university to experience an exclusive learning environment and sociable atmosphere. I also expect all of your support and combined effort to impart the quality education and foster values to make a centurion light source that can illuminate our beloved Bangladesh and the globe as well. Thank you all.   Read more >>

Prof. Dr. Al-Nakib Chowdhury
Honorable Vice-Chancellor
